My hobbies

Quitar

I love playing the guitar because it allows me to express my emotions and feelings. Music fills me with inspiration and helps me relax. Each melody is a new story, and I enjoy creating music both alone and with friends. The guitar opens up a world of different styles and directions, helping me develop as a musician. It is not just a hobby, but an important part of my self-expression.

Dombyra

I love playing the dombyra because it is connected to my culture and traditions. The sounds of the dombyra fill me with warmth and bring back memories of my family. Playing this instrument allows me to convey emotions and tell stories. It is not just a hobby, but a way to preserve and convey our cultural identity.
